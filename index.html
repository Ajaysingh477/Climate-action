Case Study: The Disappearing Sundarbans

Location: West Bengal, India & Bangladesh border

What's happening: Islands are sinking. Ghoramara Island has lost 70% of its land since the 1960s.

Impact: 7,000 people forced to relocate. Fishing communities lost their livelihoods. Children can't attend school regularly.

Science: Sea level rise combined with land subsidence. Mangrove forests can't keep pace.
